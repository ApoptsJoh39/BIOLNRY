A collective consciousness, spreading from person to person, in pursuit of reducing environmental pollution. Life and Energy!

Our Story

Founded in 2025, our Marketplace was born with a simple idea: to create a shopping platform that rewarded customer loyalty with better prices on high-quality clothing. We observed that most e-commerce sites charge the same prices regardless of customer status, missing the opportunity to build stronger relationships with their buyers.

We've created a revolutionary three-tiered pricing system that offers the best deals to our business partners. Those seeking quality at factory prices can rely on us. Our business partners receive exclusive 25% discounts on all products in our catalog. Plus, we're preparing a promotion with 35% off participating products one day a week. Coming soon!

The pollution of the Zahuapan and Atoyac rivers in Tlaxcala and Puebla, caused by the textile industry, is a serious problem. We are committed to actively contributing to its reduction. Since our launch, we have helped thousands of customers save money by connecting them directly with manufacturers for very affordable prices. We offer products that respect water sustainability and the surrounding ecosystem. Furthermore, we are dedicated to guiding and supporting our manufacturers so they can continuously improve and adhere to ISO 14001 and 9001 standards, as this is a crucial requirement to be part of our network. As we build a community of loyal customers who value our transparent pricing structure and the quality of our products—the result of healthy internal competition among manufacturers—we maintain our fundamental commitment: we will only sell products from manufacturers who operate functioning textile wastewater treatment plants.
